Our Story

The Brand Legacy

Haldiram's love for bhujia started in Bikaner, but Nagpur became its forever home in 1970. It was here that they built their first factory, bringing even more delicious snacks and sweets to India. Family owned, Haldiram's commitment to quality and traditional flavors has never wavered. Nagpur is where they continue to share the taste of India with the world, growing from their roots here into a brand loved for its tasty treats.
