Our Story

The Brand Legacy

Over the past eight decades, we've expanded, introduced new products, opened retail chains across India, and entered new markets overseas. Despite these changes, we remain a tight-knit family business dedicated to offering the most authentic taste of India.

Our journey began with Ganga Bishan Agarwal (Haldiram Ji) in a small namkeen shop in Bikaner. This shop quickly gained popularity for its unique bhujia. His grandson, Shiv Kishan Agrawal, has since propelled the business to new heights.
